SENSITIVITY REDUCTION: The Third Pillar of Personal Resilience


﻿


In our journey toward holistic well-being, understanding and managing our emotional responses is paramount. Building upon the foundational concepts of Coping Capacity and Adaptability, the third pillar—Sensitivity Reduction—offers insights into refining our emotional resilience.



What Is Sensitivity Reduction?

Sensitivity Reduction involves the conscious effort to modulate our emotional reactions to external stimuli. It's not about suppressing feelings but about cultivating a balanced response to life's challenges. By doing so, we prevent emotional overwhelm and maintain our inner equilibrium.
