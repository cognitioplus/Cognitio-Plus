ABOUT US

OUR STORY


Founded in 2020, Cognitio+ emerged as a response to the mental health crisis exacerbated by COVID-19,  inspired by the Mental Health Act of 2018, and WHO-aligned frameworks. The company was established by a group of passionate professionals who recognized the urgent need for accessible and effective mental health services, particularly in underserved communities, local government untis, organizartions, and institutions.
